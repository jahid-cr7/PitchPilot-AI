## Recording Tips

### Setup
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 minimum; 1440p recommended for crisp text
- **Browser:** Chrome or Edge at full width; hide bookmarks bar
- **Zoom:** 100% browser zoom (no scaling) so text is readable
- **Cursor:** Use a visible, high-contrast cursor (e.g., macOS pointer放大 or Windows pointer trails disabled)
- **Mic:** Use a quiet room or noise-cancelling mic; avoid keyboard tapping

### During Recording
- **Move slowly** — pause 1–2 seconds after each click before scrolling
- **Narrate everything** — don't rely on viewers reading silently
- **Show, don't tell** — click buttons instead of just hovering
- **Avoid dead air** — keep talking or cut pauses in post
- **No personal data** — ensure no real API keys, filenames, or photos are visible

### Post-Production
- **Trim start/end** — remove the "Start Recording" moment and trailing silence
- **Add captions** — YouTube auto-captions or manual SRT for accessibility
- **Export settings:**
  - Format: MP4 (H.264)
  - Resolution: 1920×1080
  - Frame rate: 30 fps
  - Bitrate: 8–12 Mbps for crisp text

## What to Say on Each Page

### Home Page
> "PitchPilot AI is an AI interview and presentation coach. The home page shows the feature set, practice modes, and a one-click demo mode for instant testing."

### Practice Page
> "Here you pick a practice mode and question. We cover software engineering, AI/ML, data analysis, university admissions, presentations, sales, and behavioral interviews. The target role auto-fills, and the random question picker keeps things fresh."

### Feedback Page
> "The feedback page shows your analysis results. Video intelligence gives motion and metadata. Camera presence checks if you're visible and well-framed. Speech analytics transcribes your audio and counts filler words."

### AI Coach
> "The AI Coach evaluates your answer content — structure, relevance, and clarity. It works offline with a rule-based engine I built, or upgrades to real LLM analysis when you add an API key. Both modes return the same structured feedback."

### Final Score
> "The final score combines video, camera, speech, and answer into a weighted overall rating. You get strengths, weak points, and a specific next task based on your weakest area."

### Dashboard
> "The dashboard tracks your improvement over time. You see total sessions, average score, best score, and a trend line. Component breakdowns show which dimensions need work."

### History
> "Your session history lets you inspect any past practice, review the full transcript, and export professional reports in HTML or CSV format."

### Settings
> "Settings shows your current AI mode. You can add a temporary API key for this session only — it's never saved to disk. The health check verifies your provider connection with one click."
